Now is the time to connect the St. Louis region via the North South MetroLink expansion.

 
 

The time to build a more robust regional transit network is NOW!

St. Louisans love MetroLink. Both city and county residents have voted for to fund it with additional tax dollars many times. The last initiative, Prop A in 2010 has gone unfulfilled. Money has been accumulating and is not going towards its intended purpose. We need a concerted, sustained effort to move this project forward.

The current MetroLink has been a huge success since opening in 1993, exceeding ridership projections and stimulating over $16 billion in development within a 10 minute walking distance of the existing MetroLink route with additional development in the works.

The North South Route is the Best Route

East-West Gateway has been working since 2000 to determine the best expansion and the North South line has been consistently identified as the best route. Now is the time to move ahead with the proposed North South MetroLink route from Florissant Valley Community College in Ferguson, through North City, South City all the way to South County mall via our vibrant downtown. MetroLink reliably connects a diverse labor force with an increased number of employers. Transit brings investment, returns dollars to the local community and promotes growth.
